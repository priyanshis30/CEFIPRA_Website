Two governments, one committee structure

CEFIPRA is jointly steered by the Department of Science & Technology, Government of India, and the Ministry for Europe and Foreign Affairs, Government of France. The chart below traces reporting lines from the Governing Body down to the Director.

Apex governing authority Governing Body (GB) View members of the Governing Body →
FSC Finance Sub-Committee View members →

All three bodies report directly to the Governing Body, which holds ultimate oversight of CEFIPRA's bilateral programmes.
